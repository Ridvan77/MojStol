Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MojStol restaurant reservation platform

MojStol makes it easy to find and book restaurants. Simply search for restaurants by location or cuisine type, select your preferred dining time and party size, and confirm your reservation. You'll receive an immediate confirmation email, and the restaurant will be notified of your booking.

Yes, making reservations through MojStol is completely free for diners. We believe in providing a convenient service without any additional costs to our users.

You can easily modify or cancel your reservation through your MojStol account. Simply log in, go to 'My Reservations', and select the booking you wish to change. Please note that some restaurants may have specific cancellation policies.

If you're running late, please contact the restaurant directly or update your reservation through our platform. Most restaurants will hold your table for 15 minutes, but policies may vary by establishment.

Yes, you can add special requests (such as dietary requirements or seating preferences) when making your reservation. Restaurants will do their best to accommodate your requests, though they cannot always be guaranteed.

After dining at a restaurant booked through MojStol, you'll receive an email invitation to leave a review. You can also log into your account and rate restaurants from your reservation history.

If you need assistance, you can contact our support team through the 'Help' section of our website, email us at support@mojstol.ba, or call our customer service line during business hours.
