Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MojStol. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Reservation details (Date, time, party size, special requests)
  • Account credentials (encrypted)
  • Usage data (How you interact with our platform)
  • Device information (Browser type, IP address, device type)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and manage your restaurant reservations
  • Communicate reservation confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services
  • Send relevant notifications and updates (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraudulent activities

4. Information Sharing

We share your information with:

  • Restaurants where you make reservations (only necessary booking details)
  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure data storage
  • Limited access to personal information

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request data portability
  • Object to data processing

7. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our platform. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies for platform functionality
  • Analytics cookies to improve our service
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
