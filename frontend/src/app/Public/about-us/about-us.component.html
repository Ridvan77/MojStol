About MojStol

Simplifying restaurant reservations across Bosnia and Herzegovina

Our Story

Founded in 2024, MojStol emerged from a simple idea: making restaurant reservations hassle-free for both diners and restaurant owners. We recognized the need for a modern, efficient solution to connect food lovers with their favorite dining spots.

What started as a local initiative in Sarajevo has grown into a trusted platform serving restaurants and diners across Bosnia and Herzegovina.
